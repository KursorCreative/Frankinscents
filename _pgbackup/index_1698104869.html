Discover the art of luxury with FrankinSents organic soy candles

Welcome to FrankinSents, your premier destination in Christchurch, New Zealand, for exquisite handcrafted organic soy candles and melts. Nestled in the bustling heart of the city, we take pride in offering an unparalleled range of candles that seamlessly blend quality, sustainability, and a touch of opulence into your daily life. Our journey as enthusiastic entrepreneurs is fueled by a mission to transform the candle industry, advocating for eco-friendly practices and unwavering in our commitment to excellence.  

Eco-Friendly elegance in every candle

At FrankinSents, each candle in our collection is a testament to our dedication to the environment and our craft. Crafted from 100% organic soy wax, our candles ensure a clean and prolonged burn, minimizing your ecological footprint while maximizing your experience of luxury. We understand the importance of sustainability, and our commitment to eco-friendly practices is evident in every hand-poured candle we create.
